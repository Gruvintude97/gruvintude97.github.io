Frequently Asked Questions

How do i know this ruler is accurate?

To test the size of the ruler, you can compare it's length with real world objects. For example, the diameter of a quarter is 0.955in. Place a quarter on the device displaying the ruler, and if the ruler displays a diameter of 0.955in, the ruler is accurate.

How do i measure objects with the ruler?

To measure objects with the ruler, you might place an object on the device displaying the ruler, or you might place the object beside the device that is displaying the online ruler.

How do i see more of the ruler (on smaller screens)?

To view more of the ruler, vertical or horizontal, you must scroll to the right, or scroll down.
